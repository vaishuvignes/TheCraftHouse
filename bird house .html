DIY Bird House

A very easy yet beautiful DIY bird house tutorial for home decor. You can make
this creative craft idea using recycled craft materials at home such as waste
cardboard, ice cream sticks, scrap pieces of craft foam sheet and a few basic craft
and stationary items. This home decoration craft project is quite simple and
budget friendly and the best part is that it is so pretty and colorful that it will instantly lift up the look of your room, balcony or wherever it is placed

Materials Required :

  1. Cardboard

  2. Cardstock/ Chart Paper

  3. Craft Foam Sheet

  4. Popsicle Sticks

  5. Ruler

  6. Pencil

  7. Scissors

  8. Cutter

  9. Glue

Instructions:

Watch video tutorial on how to make DIY Bird House.

  1. Take a cardboard sheet cutout in a shape as shown in the picture.

  2. With help of a cutter, make creases on the margin from the edges of the
    cutout for folding.

  3. Now fold the cardboard sheet along the creases in the inward direction.
    This will roughly form the base and three sides of the bird house.

  4. For the front part of the bird house, take another pentagon shaped cardboard sheet and cutout a round entrance hole as shown in the picture.

  5. Stick both the cardboard cutouts to form the basic structure of the house.

  6. Now stick the cardstock or chart paper to cover the outside walls of this
    house structure.

  7. Stick two thin strips of cardboard in between the two slanting edges on the upper side to make support for the roof.

  8. Now stick the colorful popsicle sticks, one at a time, from the top center
    point along the slanting edge to make a thatched roof.

  9. Once the basic house with roof is done, it’s time to decorate. For this, we will cutout a cute owl and tree branch with leaves using craft foam sheet.

  10. Paste the owl & tree motif on the front side of the DIY bird house.

  11. Also paste some popsicle stick pieces and colorful foam sheet cutouts on the front side wall to give it a more vibrant look.












This completes our beautiful DIY bird house which is a great home décor craft
idea using recycled materials. Hang this colorful and cute bird house in your balcony or porch and you are sure to get many compliments for this pretty handmade decorative item for your home.
