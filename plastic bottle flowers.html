Plastic Bottle Flowers

Plastic Bottle Flowers – the most beautiful best out of waste craft idea. Making flowers is easily one of the most popular crafts around the globe. But what if you could make beautiful flowers from waste plastic bottles? If you are looking for some creative ways to recycle used soda bottles and other plastic bottles, this is a must try DIY craft project for you. Easy to make and can be used as decorations for events and parties..

Materials Required :

  1. Plastic Bottle

  2. Glittery Net Fabric

  3. Scissors & Cutter

  4. Stapler

  5. Cello tape

  6. Needle & Thread

  7. Beads

Instructions:

Watch Video Tutorial for Plastic Bottle Flowers

  1. Stick the cello tape around the bottle to measure similar size rings.

  2. Using paper cutter & scissors cut out 6 plastic rings.

  3. Cut strips of net fabric of around 1” width.

  4. Attach one end of the fabric to the plastic ring using cello tape.

  5. Now wrap the net fabric around the ring to fully cover it.

  6. Pinch the rings at one point to form petals of the plastic bottle flower.

  7. Using a big needle and thread, sew the petals and beads together to form a flower.

  8. In the end, staple all the petals to secure firmly.
