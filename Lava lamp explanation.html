Lava Lamp

Overview:

A lava lamp is the perfect experiment teach your child
about density.

What do you need:

  • Water
  • Vegetables oil/baby oil
  • Food colouring
  • Effervescent tablets.

Explanation:

You can substitute the effervescent tablets with:

    a pinch of baking soda and a drop of vinegar or

    a spoon of table salt.

  1. In a bottle, pour water till one-third capacity.

  2. Instrust your child to add their favourite food colour
    and mix well.

  3. Add baby oil or vegetable oil to the mix untill an inch
    or two is left at the top.

  4. You would see oil at the top and the water at the
    bottom, both staying seperate.

  5. Explain to your little one that water is heavier than oil.

Lesson learnt:

Density of diiferent liquids
