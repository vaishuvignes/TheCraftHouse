Rainbow walking water

Overview:

This walking water science experiment is so such fun and super easy to do.

Checkout the video to see how easy this walking water experiment really is.

This rainbow activity is perfect for spring science!

What do you need:

  • 3 cups of water
  • 3 different food colors

Steps:




  1. Arrange them in a circle, alternating colored and
    clear containers, abd make bridges between the container with folded paper towels and wait for
    some times.

  2. Your kids will be amazed to see the colored water
    "walk" over the bridges and into the clear containers, mixing colors, and giving them a first-hand look at the magic of capillarity.
