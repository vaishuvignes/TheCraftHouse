Melting
Snowballs

Overview:

This is a acid and base experiment, and it's fun on a couple different levels!

What do you need:

  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. First, form snowballs by adding water to the baking
    soda.

  2. You'll need about a 1/3 cup per snowfall.

  3. Then,place them in the freezer for 15 minutes.

  4. Finally, pour drop of vinegar on the snowballs and
    watch them sizzle and melt.

Variations:

You can also make a snowman and do the same thing!
