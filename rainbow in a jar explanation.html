Rainbow in a jar

What do you need:

  • 6 glasses or cups
  • Warm water 1 cup and measuring cup
  • Sugar and measuring tspoon
  • Food coloring
  • Spoon and baster
  • Test tubes

Steps:

  1. Set out 6 glasses.

  2. Measure 1 cup of water into each glass.

  3. This is a great time to explain the importance of all the glasses having the same amount of water!

  4. Add a few drops of food coloring to each glass of water.

  5. You could have your child mix the colors or help them with mixing the colors!

  6. Measure and add a different amount of sugar to each glass of colored water.

  7. Here we use only 6 colors, you want more colors, just use it.

  8. Stir until as much of the sugar is dissolved as possible.

    9. Note:

    From experience we have found 6 colors is the easiest
    to work with!




  9. Time to use your baster or pipette to create a colorful rainbow in a jar.

  10. Squeeze the baster and put it in the red water. Release
    a little of the pressure to suck up some red water.

  11. Keeping it squeezed, transfer to orange, release a little more to suck up some orange water.

  12. Continue to do this fpr all the colors. Make sure you
    leave enough pressure in the baster to get you through all six colors

    13. Finally, you will get rainbow in a jar.
