Cute Pen Stand DIY

An extremely easy and cute cupcake pen stand DIY for you. This pretty craft idea
is great for keeping your stationery and desk organized and clutter free. And because of its vibrant and fun look, this cute pencil holder or organizer is more interesting for kids. You can use this cupcake pen stand DIY to organize your
kid’s stationery like pencils, sketch pens, color pens and other items as well which we bet, they are going to enjoy. What is more interesting is that this DIY organizer is made by recycling waste cardboard and transformed into a beautiful kawaii cupcake pen holder using colorful craft foam sheets. You can easily make this desk organizer by following some quick steps given below.

Materials Required :

  1. Cardboard

  2. Foam sheet

  3. Paint and brush

  4. Pencil

  5. Ruler

  6. Scissors

  7. Distress Ink

  8. Glue gun

Instructions:

Watch video tutorial for cute cupcake pen stand DIY

  1. On a cardboard, draw and cut out 2 squares of size 7cm * 7cm, and 2 rectangles of size 5cm * 7cm.

  2. Paint all the cutouts using white acrylic paint and a paint brush.

  3. Now for base, cut out a rectangle of size 5cm* 6.8 cm and paint it.

  4. Paste all the above parts together to make cardboard box as shown in the pictures.

  5. Cover all the sides of box with white foam sheet.

  6. Also take a thin foam strip and stick it along the neck of the box to give it a more finished look.

  7. Now, draw and cut out the cup shape or base of the cupcake on a blue foam sheet.

  8. Also, draw and cut out two frosting layers for the cupcake, on a pink and yellow foam sheet as shown in the picture.

  9. For the toppings, draw and cutout some sprinkles and cherries.

  10. To make the cupcake look more realistic and 3D, dab some distress ink along the edges of all parts of the cupcake.

  11. Now to decorate the box, first stick the blue cake cutout on the longer side of the box as shown in the picture.

  12. Next, stick the pink and yellow frosting layers on top of the cake, as shown in the picture.

  13. And then, stick the cherry on top and the sprinkles spread all over the yellow frosting.

  14. To add more detailing, make vertical lines on the base of the cupcake to give
    it a more realistic look.

  15. Similarly, decorate the opposite side of the box using craft foam sheet
    cutouts, and with that the cute cupcake pen stand DIY is complete.
