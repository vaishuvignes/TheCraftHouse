Homemade
Slime

Overview:

What kid doesn't love slime?

This is one of our favourite science experiments because
it's a sure kid-pleaser! Slime has quickly become one of those classic science experiments that every family should try at least once!

What do you need:

  • Water
  • White school glue
  • Food coloring
  • Borax
  • Two bowls

Steps:

  1. First, mix 1/4 cup of water and 1/4 cup of white school glue in a bowl. (Double the recipe if you'd like more.)

  2. Next, if you want your slime to be colorful, add a few drop of food coloring to the mixture.

  3. Then, mix 1/2 tablespoon of borax and 1/2 a cup of water and stir it around until the borax dissolves.

  4. Finally, add the borax solution to the glue mixture and mix them together.

Variations:

For glow in the dark slime, use glow-in-the-dark glue. For glittery slime, add glitter!
