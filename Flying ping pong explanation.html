Flying Ping
Pong Ball

Overview:

Your kids will love this flying ping pong trick, and there are only two things you need.

What do you need:

  • Ping pong ball
  • Hair dryer

Steps:

  1. First, make sure your hair dryer is on a cool setting.

  2. Then, hold the ping pong above the nozzle and turn on the air.

  3. When you let the ping pong go, it should float!Next,
    try it on different settings.

  4. Try to see how far you can tilt your hair dryer before
    the ball drops.
