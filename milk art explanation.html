Milk Art

What do you need:

  • A plate
  • Half a cup of milk
  • Dishwashing liquid
  • Cotton buds
  • Three food colors

Steps:

  1. Instruct your child to carefully pour the milk on the
    plate.

  2. Once the milk is still, add drops of different food
    colors and add drop of dishwashing liquid into it and using bud touch the colors in the milk.

    3. Cue "WOW"

  3. Explain to your child that the milk has fat in it and the colors float on the top because they are lighter.

  4. When the dishwashing liquid comes in contact with the milk, the fat separates making colorful patterns.

  5. This is why dishwashing liquid is used to clean oily and greasy utensils.
