Jumping Pepper

Overview:

This simple experiment will demonstrate static electricity right before your eyes.

What do you need:

  • Plate
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hair comb

Steps:

  1. Place a table spon of pepper on a plate.

  2. Then, run a hair comb through your hair.

  3. While still holding on the comb, hold the comb over
    the plate and watch the pepper jump!

What's happening?

  • The plastic comb builds up a negative charge as
    it moves through you hair.

  • The pepper has a positive charge, so it jumps!

Variations:

Try it with other spices and powders.
