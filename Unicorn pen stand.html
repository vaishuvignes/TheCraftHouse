DIY Unicorn Pen Stand

A beautiful DIY Unicorn Pen Stand idea for storage and organization of stationery
items made using waste cardboard and craft paper. This cute unicorn pen stand
can be used to store pens, pencils and other small stationary items to keep your
desk / work area clean and organized. Making this DIY unicorn pen stand is really
easy and can also be a great fun DIY and craft activity for the children and grown ups. All you require to make this pen stand is some waste cardboard , white paper and some basic stationary items given below.

Materials Required :

  1. Cardboard

  2. Marker Pen

  3. White Paper

  4. Paint

  5. Paint Brush

  6. Pencil

  7. Scissor / Paper Cutter

  8. Glue

Instructions:

Watch the video tutorial for making DIY unicorn pen stand at home.

  1. Take 2 pieces of cardboard measuring 15 cm * 9 cm and draw the outline of a cloud

  2. Using a paper cutter, cutout the shape of cloud.

  3. Using the cardboard cutout, trace the shape of cloud on the white sheet of paper.

  4. Now cut out 4 such cloud shapes from the white paper.

  5. Stick these white cloud cutouts on either side of the cardboard cloud as
    shown.

  6. Taking another piece of cardboard, cut of 2 squares measuring 5 cm * 5 cm
    and cover them using white paper.

  7. Similarly cut out a rectangle measuring 8 cm * 5 cm and also cover with white paper.

  8. Take a small piece of white paper and paint it pink. Once dry, cut out small hearts from this painted paper.

  9. Similarly, paint another piece of paper using different colors as shown below.

  10. Once dry, cut out two horn shapes from this paper.

  11. Now take the two white cloud parts and draw the eyes and mouth using a marker pen.

  12. Also stick the horn and the hearts as cheeks of the unicorn.

  13. Assemble all the pieces and stick together using glue.

  14. The cute DIY unicorn pen stand is now ready.
