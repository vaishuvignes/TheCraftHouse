Bubbles

Overview:

Now that you know a little science behind bubbles, you
can easily make your own!!

What do you need:

  • 3 cups of water (distilled is best if you have some)

  • 1 cup of liquid dish soap (dawn or joy brands work
    best)

  • 1/2 cup of white corn syrup OR 1/2 cup of glycerin
    (these ingredients will slow down water evaporation
    and help build stronger bubbles)

  • A container to keep your bubble solution in

Steps:




  1. Pour the water into your container.

  2. Add the glycerin or corn syrup to the container and
    stir.

  3. Carefully pour and gently stir the dish soap into the container.

  4. For best results, let the bubbles solution sit overnight.

  5. You are now ready to blow some bubbles! Grab your bubble wands and get outside!
