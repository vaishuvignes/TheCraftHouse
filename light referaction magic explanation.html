Light Refraction Magic

Overview:

The simple experiment

What do you need:

  • Piece of paper
  • Marker
  • Glass
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Get a sheet of paper and draw two big arrow on it,
    one near the top and one new the bottom.

  2. Make the arrow in the same direction.

  3. Next, fill a glass with water.

  4. Slowly lower the piece of paper behind the class of
    water.

  5. Look through the glass of water and watch in.
