Materials Required :

  1. Waste Plastic Bottle

  2. Scissors / Cutter

  3. Coloured Tape

  4. Marker Pen

  5. Acrylic Paint & Brush

  6. Thread & Needle

Instructions:

Watch video tutorial for Plastic Bottle Christmas Decoration.

  1. Wrap the tape around the plastic bottle to mark the edges for cutting.

  2. Cut a ring of around 1-inch width.

  3. Smoothen out the edges of the ring by gently moving it on the hot iron surface.

  4. From the leftover plastic bottle, cut out stars of three different sizes.

  5. Paint the plastic stars using acrylic paint.

  6. Punch 6 holes on the plastic ring to tie the stars.

  7. Using a needle and thread attach the stars on the ring as shown.

  8. Tie all the loose threads together to make the plastic bottle Christmas decoration.








We have used acrylic paint in pearl finish to colour the stars which makes it look really elegant. But if you want to make it more festive and brighter you can also
use glitter to make the stars in this plastic bottle Christmas decoration DIY. Once you are done just hang it around the house or make a smaller version to use as Christmas ornaments for your Christmas tree decorations.
